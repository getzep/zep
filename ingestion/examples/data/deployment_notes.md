# ROBOT-101 pilot notes

Morgan Lee recorded these notes during the Clearwater Fulfillment pilot at
South Warehouse.

## Deployment

Four ROBOT-101 units were commissioned on May 6. Each unit reports status,
utilization, and alert history to OPERATIONS-DASHBOARD. The operations team
reviews the dashboard at the start of every shift.

## Calibration event

Unit three reported a calibration warning after scheduled maintenance. Riley
Chen found that the calibration record was older than the maintenance event and
ran the remote calibration workflow. The unit completed a 48-hour verification
period without another warning.

## Dashboard access

Two shift supervisors received read-only access to the utilization view. Morgan
Lee confirmed that both accounts could inspect per-unit metrics without changing
equipment records.
