# Alder Ridge Robotics — Company Handbook

## Purpose

Alder Ridge Robotics designs automation systems for fulfillment centers. The
company's current product portfolio includes ROBOT-101, ROBOT-202, and the
OPERATIONS-DASHBOARD monitoring service.

## Organization

Avery Brown is vice president of engineering and works from North Campus.
Blake Carter leads the ROBOT-202 program. Casey Nguyen is chief technology
officer and owns the software portfolio, including OPERATIONS-DASHBOARD.

## Robotics portfolio

ROBOT-101 is the company's standard picking arm. ROBOT-202 is the next model
in the product line and was called PROTOTYPE-202 during early development.
Internal documentation should use ROBOT-202 as the canonical name.

## Operations software

OPERATIONS-DASHBOARD provides equipment status, utilization, and alert history.
Engineering owns release quality, while customer operations manages access and
onboarding.

## Supplier relationship

Westbridge Components supplies the ACTUATOR-09 assembly used by ROBOT-202.
Devon Brooks manages the account. Component orders require a six-week lead time,
and engineering must approve substitutions before purchase orders are issued.

## Fulfillment evaluation

Clearwater Fulfillment operates four ROBOT-101 units at South Warehouse. Morgan
Lee manages the site evaluation and coordinates weekly reviews with Alder Ridge
Robotics. Evertrail Freight Group uses the same support process for its regional
operations team.

## Information handling

Operational reports are shared only with project members. Do not place access
tokens, credentials, or personal contact information in project notes. Report
security concerns to the engineering lead.
