# First-Time Home Buyer Checklist

## Before You Start
- Check your credit score (aim for 620+ for conventional loans, 580+ for FHA)
- Get pre-approved for a mortgage before touring homes
- Determine your budget including property taxes, insurance, and HOA fees

## Making an Offer
- Research comparable sales (comps) in the neighborhood
- Include an earnest money deposit, typically 1-3% of the purchase price
- Common contingencies: inspection, appraisal, and financing

## Closing Process
- Home inspection usually costs $300-$500
- Closing costs typically run 2-5% of the loan amount
- Final walkthrough should be done 24-48 hours before closing
