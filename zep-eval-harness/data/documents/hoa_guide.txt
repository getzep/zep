Understanding Homeowners Association (HOA) Fees

What HOA Fees Cover:
HOA fees typically cover maintenance of common areas, landscaping, community amenities like pools and fitness centers, exterior building maintenance, and sometimes utilities like water and trash. Some HOAs also cover road maintenance and snow removal.

Typical Fee Ranges:
Single-family homes: $100-$300 per month
Townhomes: $200-$400 per month
Condominiums: $250-$500 per month

What to Ask Before Buying in an HOA Community:
- Request the HOA's financial statements and reserve fund balance
- Ask about any planned special assessments
- Review the CC&Rs (Covenants, Conditions, and Restrictions) carefully
- Check if there are rental restrictions if you plan to rent the property later
